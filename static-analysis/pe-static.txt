ENTROPY ANALYSIS
Entropy scale is 0.0 meaning uniform to 8.0 meaning maximum randomness.
Encrypted or compressed data typically scores above 7.5.
The .rsrc section at 7.9 strongly indicates an encrypted payload.

WannaCry embeds a password-protected ZIP in the .rsrc section containing:
  tasksche.exe          the ransomware encryption payload
  taskhsvc.exe          the bundled Tor client
  @WanaDecryptor@.exe   the ransom note GUI
  r.wry s.wry t.wry     key and configuration files

The ZIP password WNcry@2ol7 was found during strings analysis.
All components were confirmed extracted to disk during Phase 4 dynamic analysis.
